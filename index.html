Spectrum Buddy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Spectrum Buddy respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data within the Spectrum Buddy mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name (if provided) to manage your account.
  • App Content: Task data, emergency contact details, and trigger tracking information. This data is stored locally on your device to provide the app's core features.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat and Apple In-App Purchases to manage subscriptions. We do not see or store your credit card information.

3. How We Use Information

To provide features, manage subscriptions, and improve the app.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Spectrum uses Apple In-App Purchases and RevenueCat.
